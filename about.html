Education

2011–2017

I studied at SD Negeri 183 Garanta in my hometown, Ujung Loe, Bulukumba, South Sulawesi. During elementary school, my favorite subjects were mathematics and drawing. I enjoyed solving math problems and expressing creativity through sketches. Back then, I dreamed of becoming an architect because I loved designing buildings and playing with ideas on paper. Those early years shaped my curiosity and passion for learning.

2017–2020

I continued my education at SMP Negeri 10 Bulukumba in my hometown. During these years, I was actively involved in the PMR (Palang Merah Remaja) organization, where I learned about teamwork, first aid, and social responsibility. My favorite subject remained mathematics, as I enjoyed solving complex problems and challenging myself with numbers. Middle school was a time of growth, new experiences, and building valuable skills that shaped my journey ahead.

2020–2023

I attended SMA Negeri 9 Bulukumba, where I chose the Science major (IPA). During these years, I deepened my interest in logical thinking and problem-solving, especially in mathematics and science. High school was a time of academic challenges, self-discovery, and preparation for the next stage of my education. It shaped my perspective and strengthened my determination to pursue higher learning.

2023–Present

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Digital Business at Universitas Negeri Makassar. My studies focus on the intersection of technology and business, where I learn about e-commerce, digital marketing, data analytics, and business innovation. Beyond academics, I am actively involved in Himpunan Mahasiswa Bisnis Digital (HIMA BISDIG), a student organization that allows me to develop leadership skills, expand my network, and collaborate with like-minded individuals. University life has been an exciting journey of learning, growth, and new experiences.
